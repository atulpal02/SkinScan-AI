Acne is a common skin condition involving clogged pores, pimples, and inflammation.

Melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer. It often appears as an irregular mole with asymmetry, uneven borders, multiple colors, and changes over time.

Nevus (mole) is usually benign and appears as a small, dark spot on the skin.

Warning signs of melanoma include:
- Asymmetry
- Border irregularity
- Color variation
- Diameter > 6mm
- Evolution over time

If any lesion changes rapidly, medical consultation is recommended.
